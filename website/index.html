WE PROUDLY LAUNCH OUR NEW MOBILE APP

RAKSHAK - Be protected from Road accidents

This application is based on the fact that most of the road accidents in non-urban areas go undetected due to lack of surveillance and most of the fatalities are a result of help not arriving on time.

We aim to use Nodemcu (ESP8266) microcontroller in combination with sensors like accelerometer, alcohol detector, gas detector module & DHT sensor to prevent and detect accidents in non-urban areas and this system will be linked to a mobile application and will notify the concerned person in case of any mishap.

We Introduce You Our Features

The system will be able to prevent accidents by detecting drowsiness, seatbelt status using image processing and if the person is intoxicated and will send immediate notification to the concerned individual and raise an immediate alarm in the car to avoid accidents.

In addition the system will also be able to detect crashes, flips and car roll-overs, smokes and fire due to engine failure or any electrical failure.

And will be able to call for help by sending a message and GPS location of the victim to the concerned person and local authority.
